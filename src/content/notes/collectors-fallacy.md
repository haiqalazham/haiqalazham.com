---
title: "Collector's fallacy"
updated: "2026-06-12"
---

The idea that collecting things may be more wasteful because you’re collecting for the sake of collecting. You’re not actually doing anything with it, so it ends up being dead storage.

An argument against commonplace books or resonance calendar?
